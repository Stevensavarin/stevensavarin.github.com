About Valencia, Carabobo

Population (approximate): 1,720,000

Valencia Area Size: 1,555 sq. kilometers

Population Density: 1,106 individuals per square kilometer

Valley Elevation: 479 meters above sea level

Highest Point: Hilario Peak at 1,850 meters above sea level

Origins and Name

The name "Valencia" was given by Spanish settlers in 1555, naming it after Valencia, Spain. It's the capital of Carabobo state and the third largest city in Venezuela.
