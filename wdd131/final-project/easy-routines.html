Tip: Form is critical at any level of training. If you are a beginner, focus on learning proper technique before increasing weight. Dumbbell Chest Press
Tip: Use mirrors in the gym to correct your posture and make sure your movements are smooth and controlled. If possible, record your exercises for later review or seek guidance from a qualified trainer. Exercises such as the squat, deadlift and bench press are compound movements that require proper technique to avoid injury to the back, shoulders or knees.
